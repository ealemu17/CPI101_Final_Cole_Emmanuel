Mental Health

Mental Health is an important issue due to quarantine

Pandemics can be stressful. It is important to take care of your mental health during these uncertain times.

It is important to make time for yourself and check in on your mental health to make sure you are not being overwhemeled. It is also vital to know that everyones response to stress is different and you should learn your own signs.

If you are struggling seek help. This could be a loved one, family member, someone you trust, or a medical professional. A number to call if you need professional help is 1-800-662-HELP (4357).

If you need anymore information go to the CDC or use the contact page to ask us questions!

Remember, it is okay to not be okay. Don't suffer alone.
