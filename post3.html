What in Life is Most Valuable?

Mind over matter

Posted by Gadisa Measo on November 11, 2020

The Coronavirus has challengened the quesition of What do you most value? Many people find value in money, relationships, and time. I beg to differ the most valuable thing a person can possess is health. A healthy mind and a healthy body

A healthy mind and a healthy body. Why? With out control over your mind an indiviual will never be able to have the satisfaction in there soul. Many wealthy celebrities commit suicide because the may have a high income but failed to maintain the most valuable thing... there mind.

“You have only one mind and one body for the rest of your life. If you aren't taking care of them when you're young, it's like leaving that car out in hailstorms and letting rust eat away at it" Buffett says.

Warren Buffet prioritizes mental and phyiscal health because that determines an individuals quality and length of life

I challenge people to meditate at least 15 minutes every day visualize what you want to accomplish and see that accomplishment as done. As well as excerise at least 60 mins everyday to maintain your phyiscal body. If your schedule can not accomadate 60 mins at least a 10 minute intensive workout. Your ethier growing or dieing choose today which side you will be on and take care of your health!!!

5 Healthy Habits

1. Remember Your Dream- "When you dream, your subconscious mind processes solutions to problems you work on in the daytime, but most people forget them"( Jim Kwik).

2. Drink Water- Water essential for hydration and release of fluids

3. Meditate- for at least 15 minutes clear your mind and then visualize what you want to accomplish.

4. Excercise- Do push ups, burbees,situps and work on the shorter the time the harder the intensity.

5. Cold Shower- "Cold therapy works for me and helps to reset my nervous system"(Jim Kwik).
